Cryptocurrency Ratio Tracker

About This App

This web app tracks the ratio between AXS/USDT and EOS/USDT, as well as AXS/USDT and FIL/USDT pairs on Binance every 1 minutes. It provides real-time updates and displays the current ratios.

The app also sends email alerts to buy or sell AXS, EOS, or FIL based on the calculated ratios, helping you make informed trading decisions.
